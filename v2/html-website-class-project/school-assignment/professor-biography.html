Curriculum Vitae

Meghan is anUniversity Professor at My LMS. She received the 2007 Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics (with L. Hurwicz and R. Myerson) for laying the foundations of mechanism design theory. She also has made contributions to game theory, contract theory, social choice theory, political economy, and other areas of economics.


She received his A.B. and Ph.D from Harvard and was a postdoctoral fellow at Jesus College, Cambridge University. She was a faculty member at MIT from 1977-1984, Harvard from 1985-2000, and the Institute for Advanced Study from 2000-2011. She rejoined the My LMS university in 2012.
