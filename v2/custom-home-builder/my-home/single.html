100 Vine Street, Irvine CA, 92020

Beautiful 2,888 sq. ft. home located in the gated community of Fairways in Redhawk. This 4 bedroom home has upgraded tile and high-end, rich laminate floors downstairs for easy cleaning and beautiful living. Large kitchen with upgraded, enlarged island is perfect for gatherings. Stainless steel appliances and granite counters complete the upgraded kitchen. Kitchen opens to great room with fireplace and back yard. All bedrooms are large with extra large closets. Master bedroom walk in closet is huge! Upstairs has built-in study area and linen cupboards. Upstairs laundry room for your convenience. Garage floors are epoxied for clean finish. Plenty of room for the whole family!
